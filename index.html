// HERITAGE & QUALITY

Built on Trust,
Crafted for Generations.

Since 2005, Benjaly Rocks has stood as a benchmark for high-performance concrete blocks and luxury architectural tiles in Chemmad, Malappuram.

What began as a trusted local yard for durable hollow bricks has grown into Malappuram's leading showroom experience, curated specifically for premium residential and commercial structures. We select only the highest grade of river sands, washed aggregates, and raw materials, guaranteeing that every block and tile delivered to your site meets rigorous structural load-bearing parameters.
